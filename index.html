vimberlin - Vim User Group Berlin ================================= Mailing List: https://groups.google.com/group/vimberlin/ Twitter: @vimberlin Calendar: iCal Next Meetup: 2012-03-22, 8 p.m. at LAUNCH/CO List of favourite plugins - 2012-01-26 -------------------------------------- * fugitive.vim - A Git front end in Vim * surround.vim - quoting/parenthesizing made simple * tmru.vim - Easy access to most recently used files * ... Please fork this document on GitHub and add your own recommendations or leave a note on the mailing list and I will add it here myself. Recap: First Meetup - 2012-01-26 -------------------------------- Thank you all for coming. It was a great evening and I had a lot of fun. I'm especially proud, that so many of you guys showed up. I think we were about 20 people. I think, that's pretty impressive for a first meeting. I would like to thank Drew for his thoughts on plugins and how they fit into the Vim universe. Also, playing some vimgolf was both entertaining and illuminating. Afterwards we were talking about our favourite plugins. I hope, we can still collect a list of all of them here for future reference. See the article above for more detail. Finally we talked about vimberlin itself. We've settled to meet on the 4th Thursday of every odd month. So the next meetup will be on March 22nd 2012 - 8 p.m. again at LAUNCH/CO We are starting to using a mailing list to discuss talk proposals and other stuff. (There is a link at the top of the page.) I've also added a link to an iCal feed containing the schedule for the next meetups. I'll continue to use the Twitter account vimberlin for announcements and things of (potentially) general interest. Thanks Jan and LAUNCH/CO for hosting the event and providing us with chairs, beers and beamer and thank you all for making vimberlin happen.
